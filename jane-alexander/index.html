Mission Statement

Conversion therapy, or the practice of trying to change an individualâ€™s sexual orientation, gender identity or gender expression, is legal in the state of North Carolina. Over 700,000 LGBTQ people have been subjected to some form of conversion therapy in their lifetime, and an estimated 80,000 LGBTQ youth will experience the horrors of this practice in the coming years.

Born Perfect NC is the campaign to protect minors and disabled adults from these harmful attempts to change an inherent part of their identity. Join us as we fight to safeguard the mental health of young people and ensure that LGBTQ North Carolinians know they are Born Perfect. .
